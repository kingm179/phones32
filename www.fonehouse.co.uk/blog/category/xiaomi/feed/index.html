]]> Who is Xiaomi?

Founded in 2010, Xiaomi is one of the newest phone brands- offering high quality handsets at low prices. Despite the company’s youth, Xiaomi already has presence in over 100 countries and regions.

Which Xiaomi Phone is Best for Me?

Xiaomi has some fabulous offerings and deciding which Xiaomi phone is best all comes down to what you’re looking for. Is your priority a killer camera that will capture perfect pics day and night? Or perhaps your dream phone is one with lightning-fast charging capabilities, or impressive battery life? You might be a mobile phone gamer whose top criteria is screen size and quality? Or perhaps you’re looking for the best phone display for your eye health?

Whatever you’re looking for in a phone, we can help you to find the perfect Xiaomi device to fit it.

Which is the Latest Xiaomi Phone?

Xiaomi’s latest flagship is the Xiaomi 12, with its variants such as the Xiaomi 12 Pro . Both the Xiaomi 12 and Xiaomi 12 Pro boast the new super powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chip and a 50MP main camera.

Which Xiaomi Phone has the Best Camera?

Xiaomi certainly delivers when it comes to cameras, with many of their handsets boasting triple and quad rear cameras. However, our pick for best Xiaomi phone for camera is the Xiaomi Mi 11.

The Mi 11’s triple lens rear camera includes a 108MP main camera, a 13MP 123-degree ultra-wide angle camera, and a 5MP telemacro camera. Plus, thanks to the camera’s Night Mode capabilities, you can continue taking phenomenal shots after dark.

The Mi 11’s camera isn’t just great for taking pictures though- it also takes some pretty impressive videos too thanks to HDR10+ Video Recording and 108 MP one-click cinema shoot. Plus, Ultra-Night video means you can record videos any time you like, light or dark.

When Does the New Xiaomi Come Out?

The latest phone in the Xiaomi 12 series, the next Xiaomi phone, is rumoured to be released in either July, August or September of 2022.

Which Xiaomi Phone Should I Buy?

There are so many amazing Xiaomi phones on the market right now. Here’s a rundown of just a few.

Xiaomi Redmi Note 10

If you’re looking to save money, this older handset has some pretty cool features despite its age. The 360-degree light sensor adjusts screen brightness to your surroundings, plus the device is low blue light certified to help you sleep.

The quad camera with Night Mode means you can take excellent pictures both before and after the sun goes down. And the IR blaster means your phone can double as a remote control!

Last but not least, the phone’s 5000mAh battery has power to get you through the day and then some, which is sure to be a hit if you’re frustrated with phones that need constantly charging.

Xiaomi Redmi 9AT

Do you hate lag? With the Redmi 9AT you won’t have that issue thanks to the MediaTek Helio G25 processor.

The AI Quad Camera on this device produces gorgeous photos, thanks to the camera’s ability to edit its own settings in accordance with the scenery of your picture. Plus, you can capture stunning images for the gram with portrait mode.

Xiaomi 11T Pro

Do you hate waiting around forever for your phone to charge? You’ll love the 11T Pro’s Hyper Charge, which can get you to 100% battery in a mere 17 minutes.

The display on this handset is superb- the 6.67” AMOLED screen is Dolby Vision Supported and has a 102Hz refresh rate. The 360-degree light sensor keeps screen brightness at the perfect level for your surroundings, and sunlight mode 3.0 means you can use your phone outdoors on a sunny day and still see your screen clearly.

Xiaomi Redmi Note 11 Pro

Do you like to play games on your phone? The Redmi Note 11 Pro’s 360Hz touch sampling rate means you won’t have to worry about your phone being slow to react and costing you a win, whilst the 120Hz refresh rate ensures visuals won’t be blurry. Plus, Liquid Cool technology stops your phone from overheating whilst you play.

This handset also comes with Eye Care Display- so you can use your phone plenty without worrying about your eye health.

Xiaomi 12 5G

Xiaomi’s latest flagship phone has so much to offer.

The charging capabilities are excellent, with 67W fast charging meaning you can reach 100% charge in just 39 minutes, plus wireless charging up to 50W. Not only that, the camera capabilities are brilliant too. The 50MP triple camera has a night mode function whilst the selfie camera comes with portrait mode.

And of course, we can’t talk about the Xiaomi 12 without mentioning the awesome Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 chip providing it with huge amounts of power.

Xiaomi 12 Pro 5G

The Xiaomi 12 Pro is an absolute gem of a phone, with almost too many amazing features to name.

Those who enjoy playing music on their phone will be enamored with the device’s quad stereo speakers, whilst gamers will adore the SmartTouch 480Hz touch sampling rate.

With a 6.73” screen and Adaptive SyncPro bringing brightness, clarity and smoothness to the display, the visuals on this device or stunning- perfect for those who enjoy watching videos on their phone.

And the cherry on top? Smart 120W Xiaomi HyperCharge gets your device to 100% charge in as littles as 24 minutes.
