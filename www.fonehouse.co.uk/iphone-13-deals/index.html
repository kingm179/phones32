Q. Does the iPhone 13 support 5G?

Yes, absolutely! In fact Apple themselves proudly state that no one does 5G like iPhone, which means everything from streaming to downloading happens so much faster. It’s perfect for serious multiplayer gaming, watching a big sporting fixture on the move, sharing AR videos and much more. Smart Data mode on the iPhone 13 even has the intelligence to switch down a gear when you don’t need all that speed, in order to preserve battery power.

Q. What does the iPhone 13 look like?

Featuring the most advanced dual-camera system ever seen on iPhone, the lenses have been turned 45 degrees as part of a totally new architecture. It’s protected by a Ceramic Shield, which is tougher than any smartphone glass, whilst the durable flat-edge design is both classy and convenient as it sits perfectly in the palm of your hand. Available in a variety of striking colours, including Pink, Midnight and Starlight, it’s the perfect pocket companion to help you stand out from the crowd.

Q. How do I screenshot on the iPhone 13?

Taking and sharing screenshots with family or friends is a key part of the smartphone experience, and on the iPhone 13 it couldn’t be easier. Simply press and then release both the side button and the volume up button at the same time and voila - your screenshot will be taken. You can then tap the preview in the bottom left corner to edit, save or delete your screenshot

Q. How big is the iPhone 13?

With a sizable 6.1” Super Retina XDR display, rest assured that with the iPhone 13 you can see it all in crystal clear quality, whether you’re into gaming, streaming or simply scrolling through social media. The aforementioned durable flat-edge design means it isn’t bulky however, so you need not worry about it taking up too much space in your pocket or handbag

Q. What is the iPhone 13 Cinematic Mode?

Fancy trying your hand at a bit of filmmaking? Well, the iPhone 13 went to film school, so you don’t have to! The pros use a technique called rack focus, which quickly shifts focus from one subject to another to guide the attention of their audience, and the iPhone 13 makes bringing the same storytelling capabilities to your homemade videos super easy.

Simply start recording and Cinematic mode will hold focus on your subject whilst creating stunning depth-of-field effect all around them, in Dolby Vision HDR.

Q. What are iPhone 13 MagSafe Accessories?

Add a magnetic case or wallet and get faster wireless charging with MagSafe accessories which make the whole iPhone 13 experience even better. Cases come in a huge variety of colours, sure to suit the taste of every smartphone lover, and provide an extra layer of protection to your device, whilst chargers (including handy wireless ones) and battery packs ensure you need not worry about running low on charge when going about your daily business. 

Q. What is the iPhone 13 A15 Bionic Chip?

The ultra-quick A15 Bionic provides the power behind Cinematic mode, Face ID, Photographic Styles and more, enabling the iPhone 13 to benefit from 30% faster graphics and 50% faster CPU than its competition. Simply put, A15 Bionic is speed-boosting, camera-elevating, reality-augmenting, power-optimising, privacy protecting and a truly game-changing chip which elevates the iPhone 13 right to the very top when compared with other smartphones of its kind.

Q. Can I upgrade my contract to the iPhone 13?

There are a large array of iPhone 13 deals you are able to upgrade to, however, these are only currently available to EE customers but we are working hard to expand our range as soon as possible.

Should you not be able to find an upgrade deal that suits your needs on our website, simply fill in an upgrades form and a member of our team will be in touch to talk you through a number of exclusive offers on the smartphone you love.

Q. How much will the iPhone 13 cost?

Prices for the iPhone 13 are likely to differ depending on exactly what storage size you opt for for example, with the current outright price available from around £779. This is however very reasonable when compared with other devices of its kind.

We have contracts available on fonehouse from £39 per month right now, though of course this will vary depending on the exact model and tariff you choose to purchase.

Q. What accessories come with the iPhone 13?

As with all iPhones, you will find a charger cable and SIM tray pin in the box when receiving your handset. There are a wide range of other accessories available to purchase with this smartphone too, including leather wallets, battery packs and silicone cases with MagSafe, AirTags, leather keyrings, wireless chargers and earphones - all of which make the iPhone 13 experience even better.

Q. Is the iPhone 13 Waterproof?

The iPhone 13 is water resistant and so does provide a good level of protection, however it isn’t quite completely waterproof. Apple state that the iPhone 13 is splash and water resistant, having been tested under controlled laboratory conditions with a rating of IP68 under IEC standard 60529. Essentially, it can withstand a maximum depth of 6 metres for up to 30 minutes, though you should not exceed this or expose the phone to water frequently in order to preserve its condition.
