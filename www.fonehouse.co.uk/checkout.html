What Is A PAC / STAC Code?

PAC Code

If you are out of contract and would like to keep your existing number you can now text the word PAC to 65075 for free from the number you wish to keep. Almost instantly you will be sent a PAC Code which you enter in the box provided. We will then ensure that your mobile phone number is automatically transferred to your new network within one working day.

STAC Code

If you are out of contract and would like us to cancel your existing phone contract (instead of transferring your phone number to this new contract) you can text the word STAC to 75075 for free. Almost instantly you will be sent a STAC Code which you can enter in the box provided. We will then ensure we cancel your current service as soon as your new one is connected.
